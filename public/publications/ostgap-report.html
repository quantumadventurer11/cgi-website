Outer Space Treaty Gap Analysis Project final report.

Download, cite, and understand the context for CGI's flagship treaty implementation report.

Report summary

The report studies implementation gaps between Outer Space Treaty obligations and domestic space activity frameworks. It is intended as a research contribution for readers working on space law, national authorization, supervision, non-appropriation, and harmful contamination.

How to cite
