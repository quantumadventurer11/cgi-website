Institute Focus

Policy research for the next legal frontier.

CGI studies the legal, administrative, economic, and institutional questions that arise as human activity moves from orbital systems toward sustained presence on celestial bodies.

Serious governance before facts on the ground.

Our work is designed for academic review, policy dialogue, and institutional reform pathways that preserve the rule of law before commercial and strategic competition hardens into precedent.

Modernizing the Outer Space Treaty

Gap Analysis, Comparative State Practice, and Expert Perspectives on Reform Pathways examines where the 1967 Outer Space Treaty is under strain and how practical reform can proceed without abandoning its foundational architecture.

Download PDF Publication page

Core contribution

  1. Maps interpretative and normative pressure points in the Outer Space Treaty.
  2. Compares state practice across major spacefaring jurisdictions.
  3. Frames expert consultation as qualitative input for reform pathways.

Use cases

  1. COPUOS-facing policy discussion.
  2. Academic review and citation.
  3. Future briefs on resource governance and lunar administration.
