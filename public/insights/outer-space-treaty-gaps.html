Briefing

What are gaps in the Outer Space Treaty?

Not every gap is a vacuum; some are implementation problems.

The Outer Space Treaty sets core principles, but many operational details are left to interpretation, national implementation, and later practice.

Types of gaps

Gaps may involve ambiguity in treaty language, weak domestic implementation, missing procedures, or new activity that was not anticipated when the treaty was drafted.

Why Articles II, VI, and IX recur

These provisions connect sovereignty, responsibility, authorization, supervision, environmental protection, due regard, and consultation.
