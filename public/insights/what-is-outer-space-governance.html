What is outer space governance?

A starting point for readers new to the field.

Outer space governance is the collection of treaties, national laws, institutional practices, technical standards, and policy decisions that shape activity beyond Earth.

Why the term matters

Governance is broader than law. It includes how legal duties are implemented, how agencies coordinate, how risks are reviewed, and how public-interest values are protected.

Where CGI fits

CGI studies the legal and institutional design questions that emerge when inherited rules meet new activity.
