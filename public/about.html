Governance framework

How CGI keeps research credible.

Clear charters, review standards, attribution, and escalation pathways help volunteer research become durable public work.

Project charters

Projects begin from a defined research question, scope, methodology, and leadership structure.

Publication review

Outputs released in CGI's name are reviewed for evidence, citation quality, consistency, and attribution.

Research integrity

Integrity and conduct concerns have a separate review pathway from ordinary project management.

Membership pathway

Applicants are routed toward project work or general membership based on interest, capacity, and fit.

Advisory expertise

External experts may be consulted on strategy, research quality, and publication direction.

Public accountability

CGI uses published outputs, clear contact channels, and citation standards to make its work inspectable.
