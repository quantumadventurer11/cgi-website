Why lunar governance matters before settlement

Early practices can become durable expectations.

Lunar governance matters before settlement because early decisions about access, safety, science, infrastructure, and coordination can shape later institutional expectations.

What can be planned now

Researchers can test administrative models, dispute pathways, environmental safeguards, and public reporting systems before crisis or competition forces hurried decisions.

CGI's approach

The Lunar Project uses settlement scenarios as a disciplined way to identify questions, not as a claim that any scenario is inevitable.
