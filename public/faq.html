FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Short answers for prospective members, readers, and partners.

How do I join?

Submit the form on the join page or email contact@celestialgovernance.org with your interests and relevant background.

Can I cite CGI publications?

Yes. Use the citation block on each report landing page and link to the canonical publication page where possible.

How are research outputs reviewed?

Outputs are reviewed for evidence, citation completeness, consistency, attribution, and suitability before release in CGI's name.

Can I participate without being a space lawyer?

Yes. CGI work can involve research, editing, data handling, communications, administration, and project support.

Will CGI publish more reports?

The research library is designed to grow as projects complete reviewed outputs.
