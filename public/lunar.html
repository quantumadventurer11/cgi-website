Development Horizon

2030-2100 phased development timeline.

  1. 2030s

    Surveying, legal framework design, site characterization, robotic precursors, and shared safety norms.

  2. 2040s

    First infrastructure nodes, communications relays, landing zones, and early power/logistics systems.

  3. 2050s

    Resource and logistics operations with stronger licensing, registration, and environmental monitoring.

  4. 2060s-2070s

    Permanent habitats, scientific districts, administrative services, and operational dispute-handling capacity.

  5. 2080s-2100

    Mature lunar administrative network linking Amity City, Mycenae, Hattusa, and Tycho.

Lunar Administration

A treaty-based administrative concept.

The Lunar Project explores a neutral administrative body for coordination, transparency, registry support, environmental safeguards, safety zones, and resource activity oversight. It may draw limited inspiration from international resource governance analogues while adapting them to the non-appropriation principle and the unique operating conditions of celestial bodies.

Year 2100 Layout

A network, not a capital alone.

By 2100, the four proposed nodes form a distributed governance and infrastructure network: Amity City for administration, Mycenae for science and heritage, Hattusa for power and volatile-access logistics, and Tycho for deep-field operations and resilient infrastructure.
