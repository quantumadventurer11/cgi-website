BB

Benjamin C. Brumm

Founder, Programme Lead & Secretary of the Steering Committee

Read introduction

Benjamin is a governance and policy researcher based in Halifax, Nova Scotia. He leads CGI's OST Gap Analysis Project, coordinating research on treaty gaps, property rights, and jurisdictional frameworks for extraterrestrial environments. He is completing a B.Applied Computer Science at Dalhousie University and a Diploma in Data Analytics at NSCC, and serves as a member of the Space Law and Policy Project Group at SGAC and board member of the World Federalist Movement.

CB

Charles Bird

Steering Committee Chair

Read introduction

Charles is a lecturer at Charles University Faculty of Law in Prague, where he teaches Equity and Trusts, Criminal Law, and Legal English. He is a Ph.D. candidate with a dissertation on the legal framework governing property and resource rights on celestial bodies. His research centres on the Outer Space Treaty, commercial space activities, and emerging space resource law.
