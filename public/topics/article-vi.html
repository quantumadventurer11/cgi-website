Treaty guide

Outer Space Treaty Article VI

National responsibility, authorization, and continuing supervision.

What it is

Article VI makes states internationally responsible for national activities in outer space and requires authorization and continuing supervision of non-governmental activities.

Why it matters

Private and academic actors increasingly carry out activities that still require public accountability under international law.

CGI work

OSTGAP examines how national licensing systems interpret and operationalize continuing supervision.
