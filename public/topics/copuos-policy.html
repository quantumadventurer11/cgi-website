Topic guide

COPUOS-facing policy

Research designed to be legible to international space governance deliberations.

What it is

COPUOS-facing policy work translates legal and technical research into issues, options, and language useful for multilateral discussion.

Why it matters

Good policy work separates legal claim, evidence, uncertainty, and recommendation so decision makers can evaluate each part.

Current governance gap

Emerging activities often move faster than consensus processes, making careful framing especially important.

CGI work

CGI aims to produce measured research that can support deliberation without claiming more authority than the evidence allows.
