Review standards

Project pages are public-safe research pathways.

CGI publishes research and policy analysis on outer space governance. CGI does not claim governmental, treaty-making, regulatory, licensing, diplomatic, jurisdictional, or quasi-sovereign authority. Public outputs require appropriate CGI review and sign-off.
